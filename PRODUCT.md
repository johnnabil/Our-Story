# Product Direction

## Register

Brand.

## Product Purpose

Our Story is a private, editable memory site for a couple. It should feel less like a polished marketing page and more like a memory box opened on a quiet desk.

## North Star

A handmade archive of living memories: warm, intimate, tactile, and still easy to edit.

## Audience

- The couple who owns the site and edits it over time.
- Trusted friends or family who may view it as a personal story.

## Tone

Tender, specific, unforced. Avoid wedding-template language, generic romance copy, and decorative nostalgia that does not help the story.

## Design Principles

- One physical metaphor per section.
- View mode may feel handmade; edit mode must stay calm and usable.
- Photos carry the emotion. Decoration supports them.
- Imperfection is allowed in layout, not in readability.
- Keep professional bios intimate first, professional second.

## Anti-References

- Generic scrapbook kits with tape, stickers, torn paper, and script everywhere.
- Luxury wedding palettes where gold becomes shiny trim.
- Portfolio sections that feel like LinkedIn.
- Motion that feels cute, bouncy, or toy-like.
